## Owns

- privileged Persona engine-manager operations;
- root `Operation` / `Reply` generated by `signal_channel!`;
- owner-side engine status, catalog, launch, retirement, start, and stop
  payload records;
- owner-surface round-trip tests.

## Does Not Own

- Persona daemon actors or storage;
- manager-to-child lifecycle traffic, which lives in
  `signal-persona-engine-management`;
- component-domain traffic;
- CLI parsing or socket policy.

## Invariants

- This is the owner socket vocabulary for Persona.
- Contract operations use contract-local verbs.
- Requests carry no caller identity, timestamp, sender, or authorization proof.
- Wire enums are closed and every variant has a round-trip witness.
